Card Games

The unique feature of the game is all the cards are made up of gif-memes instead of the tranditional picture cards.The game has different levels. As the level of the game increases the number of cards also increases. In the advance level timer is also introuced so that the player can play against the clock.


Fresh Veg

Fresh Veg is a website for connecting consumers and growers directly. The consumers will be able to order their fresh vegetables from the farmers. This way, the consumers will be able to get better price but also better quality vegetables as they will be freshly picked. On the other hand, the farmers will also be able to improve their profit margin due to lack of middle man which usually takes high commission fee.


Team Builder

This application is a terminal based teambuilder. The user will prompted with different roles in a team and the team members will be displayed in a webpage in card form with different colour based on their role in the team.For expemble, if they choose Manager, they will be prompted with name, employee ID, office and email address. After they fill out all the required information, a message will be shown if the process was successful or not.
